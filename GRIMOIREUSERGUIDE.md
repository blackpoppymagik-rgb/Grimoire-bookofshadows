# Book of Shadows — User Guide

*Sacred Chaos · Black Poppy Magik & Apothecary · v22*

## What this is

The Book of Shadows is your grimoire — one file, opens in any browser, works offline, lives
on your device and nowhere else. No account. No cloud. No one watching what you write.
Everything you save stays on the device you saved it on, unless you export it yourself.

*Noli illegitimi carborundum.*

## Getting started

1. Open `grimoire.html` in any browser — double-click it, or drag it into a browser window.
2. On your phone: open it in Safari or Chrome, then use "Add to Home Screen." After that it
   behaves like any other app — its own icon, opens full-screen, works with no signal.
3. That's it. No signup, no setup screen, nothing to configure.

**One thing to know:** the two files `grimoire.html` and `sw.js` travel together. If someone
sends you an update, save both files, in the same folder, replacing the old ones. Keep one
copy, and overwrite it each time — don't let your downloads fill up with `grimoire (2)`,
`grimoire (3)`, and so on. Future-you will thank present-you.

## The four rooms of the house

From the landing page, four doors:

### Enter Today's Ritual
Your daily page. The moon's current phase, the astrology season, a tarot card pulled fresh
each day (reroll it if it's not speaking to you), a rotating spell/chant/beauty
affirmation/self-care ritual/act of kindness, Witch IT lessons, witch lore, a daily sigil,
and a Word Magik writing prompt. Underneath: three journals — Ritual, Dream, and Signs &
Synchronicities — plus the Spell Basket (draw a household object, work it, record what
happened), and the Vault, where all your data lives and exports from.

### Pendulum Training
A field journal for pendulum practice — not a claim that a pendulum reveals truth, but a
place to build a real, honest practice with one. Start in **Basics** to learn technique and
watch the animated movement demo. Go to **Calibration** and let the pendulum show you *your*
yes, no, maybe, and unknown — never assume clockwise means anything in particular; that's
yours to discover. **Training** gives you six exercises (coin flip, dice, color cards, and
more) with a hidden answer and honest odds, so you can see for yourself how your practice is
going. **Journal** is for free questions — it even fills in the moon phase for you.
**Library** has short, honestly-labeled readings — history, science, and lived practice, kept
separate so you always know which is which. **Flight Recorder** is the long view: your
streaks, your patterns, your own data talking back to you. No mystical claims — just what
you've actually recorded, laid out plainly.

### Sigil Studio
A drawing table for making your own symbols. Draw freehand on the canvas — turn on Mirror or
Rotation and the page finishes what you start. **Intention** walks you through what you're
actually trying to say before you draw it, and includes a classic letter-reduction tool (type
a sentence, strike the vowels and repeats, draw from what's left). **Symbols** is a library
you can tap to stamp onto your page and then make your own — plus your own **Personal
Symbol Dictionary**, which remembers every mark you've named and used more than once.
**Methods** explains where sigils come from and how to make one that actually reads at a
glance. **Gallery** is where finished sigils live — each with its own little journal page for
what it means to you, and every save keeps the version before it, so nothing is ever really
gone.

### Pendulum Training button not there? Sigil Studio missing?
If you're looking at an older saved copy of the file, some doors won't exist yet. Check the
browser tab title — it should say **v22**. If it says v19, v20, or v21, you're holding an
earlier snapshot; ask for the current file.

### The Tarot Table
Your existing 78-card deck, now with real spreads. Pick **One Card** for a quick check-in,
**Three Card** for Past/Present/Future, the traditional ten-position **Celtic Cross** for a
real question that deserves the full anatomy, or **The Full Wheel** — twelve cards, one per
house of life, best saved for birthdays and turning points. Cards deal face-down; tap each
to turn it when you're ready. Every card opens to its Light, its Shadow, and a Black Poppy
reading underneath. No card is ever dealt reversed — you decide which voice it's speaking
with today. Save the reading with your notes, and find it again anytime in **Readings**.

## Your data, in plain language

Everything you write is stored in your browser's local storage, on the device you're using,
under the file you opened it from. It goes nowhere else — no server, no cloud, no account.

**What that means in practice:** if you clear your browser's data, or switch to a new phone
or computer, your entries won't follow you automatically. **Export regularly.** Open the
Vault (inside Today's Ritual) and download a full backup — that file is your insurance
policy. It can be imported back into any copy of the grimoire, on any device, at any time,
to restore everything exactly as it was.

There's also a CSV download if you'd rather have your entries in a spreadsheet.

## A note on what this is and isn't

None of these tools claim to predict anything, guarantee an outcome, or replace your own
judgment. The pendulum doesn't reveal objective truth — it reflects patterns in your own
practice back to you. A sigil doesn't cast a spell by existing — it holds an intention you
chose, in a shape you made. A tarot spread doesn't tell your future — it gives you a
structure to think inside of. Curiosity over certainty. Observation over assumption. The
record is the practice.

## If something goes wrong

- **A save button did nothing.** Try again outside of a private/incognito window — some
  browsers block local storage there.
- **The app looks stuck on an old version.** Close the tab completely and reopen it — the
  app caches itself for offline use, so a hard refresh sometimes helps it notice an update.
- **You're worried about where your data went.** It hasn't gone anywhere. Check the Vault
  panel any time — it shows a live count of everything saved and exactly which storage keys
  hold it.
